Welcome

👋 Hi, I'm Nick.

I’m a Customer Experience professional who helps companies create better customer interactions by combining empathy, communication, problem-solving, and technology.

Over the last five years, I’ve supported customers from around the world across technical, business, and customer-facing environments. Beyond solving customer issues, I’m interested in improving the systems behind those experiences — from designing better workflows to exploring how AI can help teams communicate more effectively and make better decisions.

My approach is simple:

Understand people. Solve problems. Build better experiences.
