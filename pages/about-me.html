Professional Background

👋 Hello! I'm Nick.

Nice to meet you! I'm a Customer Experience professional with five years of experience helping people solve problems, understand complex products, and make better decisions.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked with customers from different industries and backgrounds, often helping people navigate situations that required patience, technical understanding, and clear communication.

What I’ve learned is that great customer experience is not just about providing an answer.

It’s about understanding the person behind the question.

A customer reaching out is rarely only looking for information. They might be frustrated, confused, under pressure, or simply trying to make the right decision. My role has always been to understand that context and help create the best possible outcome.

I believe excellent customer experience comes from combining two things:

Empathy and competence.

People need to feel heard and understood — but they also need someone who can confidently guide them toward a solution.

This mindset has also influenced the way I approach technology. I created AI-assisted customer experience workflows to explore how artificial intelligence can help teams communicate better, make stronger decisions, and create more consistent customer interactions.

At my core, I’m someone who enjoys understanding problems, helping people, and constantly improving the way things work.

Beyond Work

🌱 Hi again! 😊

Outside of customer experience, I'm someone who has always been fascinated by people — how we think, what shapes our decisions, and what makes us who we are.

That curiosity is what led me to explore psychology, neuroscience, storytelling, writing, and human behaviour.

At first glance, these interests might seem unrelated. I enjoy analyzing movies, creating stories, building worlds for D&D, learning about AI, and exploring psychology. But they all connect through the same question:

Why do people become who they become?

Stories especially fascinate me because they allow us to explore identity, choices, morality, fear, growth, and transformation. This is also why I created projects like Soul Screenshots — exploring how a single cinematic moment can reveal something deeper about human experience.

I believe these interests influence the way I approach my professional work as well. Understanding people, communicating clearly, and seeing situations from different perspectives are skills that apply everywhere — whether I'm helping a customer solve a problem, designing a workflow, or building something new.

I'm naturally curious, always learning, and I enjoy connecting ideas from different fields.

Whether I'm exploring technology, studying human behaviour, or creating something just because I'm interested in it, I always come back to the same principles:

Understand more. Create better. Help people. Shine.

Psychology

Understanding what shapes people, behaviour, and motivation.

Storytelling

Exploring identity, emotion, and meaning through narrative.

Technology

Building tools that make human experiences clearer and better.

Human connection

Always coming back to the idea that people matter most.
