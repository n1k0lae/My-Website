Professional Background

👋 Hello! I'm Nick.

Nice to meet you! I'm a Customer Experience professional who loves helping people, solving problems, and learning how things work.

Over the last five years, I've had the opportunity to work in customer support roles where I helped people navigate technical issues, understand products, and find solutions when things didn't go as planned.

The thing I enjoy most about this work is the human side of it. Every customer interaction is different, and behind every question there is a person trying to achieve something. I enjoy listening, understanding what someone actually needs, and figuring out the best way to help.

I’ve always believed that great support is a combination of two things: empathy and competence. Making someone feel heard is important, but so is having the knowledge and problem-solving skills to actually make their situation better.

Outside of helping customers, I’m someone who loves learning and exploring new ideas. I'm fascinated by psychology, technology, storytelling, and the ways we can use tools like AI to improve the way people work.

That curiosity led me to create my own AI-assisted customer support workflows, exploring how AI can help people communicate better, make better decisions, and create more consistent customer experiences.

At the end of the day, I’m someone who enjoys understanding problems, helping people, and constantly improving.

Thanks for stopping by! 😊

Beyond Work

🌱 Hi again! 😊

If you couldn't already tell, I’m someone who is naturally curious.

I've always been interested in people — how they think, what shapes them, and why we make the choices we make. That curiosity is what led me to explore psychology, neuroscience, storytelling, and human behavior.

A lot of my interests might seem unrelated at first. I enjoy analyzing movies, writing stories, creating worlds for D&D, learning about AI, and exploring psychology. But when I look at them together, they all connect through one thing:

Understanding people.

Stories fascinate me because they are a way to explore what it means to be human. They let us look at identity, choices, morality, fears, and transformation.

That's also why I created projects like Soul Screenshots — to explore how a single scene, image, or moment can communicate something deeper about people and their experiences.

I love learning, creating, and connecting ideas from different fields. I’m the kind of person who can spend an afternoon learning about AI, then another analyzing a character from a story, and somehow find connections between the two.

Whether I'm helping someone solve a problem, building something new, or exploring an idea just because I'm curious, I always come back to the same thing:

Understand more. Create better. Help people.

Psychology

Understanding what shapes people, behaviour, and motivation.

Storytelling

Exploring identity, emotion, and meaning through narrative.

Technology

Building tools that make human experiences clearer and better.

Human connection

Always coming back to the idea that people matter most.
